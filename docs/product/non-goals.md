# Non-goals

The following capabilities are intentionally outside the product until a new product decision changes this document.

## Facilities and commerce

- Court inventory, availability, reservation, or conflict prevention
- Club opening hours
- Pricing, Club dues, Tournament fees, refunds, subscriptions, or payouts
- Payment-status tracking
- Equipment marketplace, sales, reviews, or inventory operations

Plain-text venue, court labels, contact details, map links, and external payment instructions are informational only.

## Communication and content

- Direct messages
- Group chat
- Comments
- Public posts or social feeds
- Arbitrary media exchange between Players

Structured requests, invitations, responses, reminders, and notifications are supported.

## Competition

- Doubles or team competition
- Leagues
- Round-robin-only, double-elimination, Swiss, Monrad, or custom Tournament formats
- Ranking systems or ranking-based seeding
- Live scoring, refereeing, timers, or point-by-point tracking
- Scored Matches inside Play Sessions in the Initial release
- Walkovers affecting Player Competition Records

## Administration

- Mobile management mode
- User impersonation
- Unrestricted Platform Administrator access to private Player or Guardian data
- Multiple simultaneous Guardians for one Junior Player

## Scope enforcement

An existing table, route, UI placeholder, dependency, or partially implemented capability does not move a non-goal or Later feature into Initial scope.
