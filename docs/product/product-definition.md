# Product Definition

## Purpose

Squash is a Costa Rica platform where Players build trusted squash relationships, organize social play, and compete, while Clubs manage membership, Club activities, and official competitions.

The mobile app serves Players. The web portal serves Club and platform management.

## Audiences

- **Players** use mobile to manage their identity, discover and join Clubs, attend Club activities, and participate in competitions.
- **Guardians** use mobile to supervise Junior Players.
- **Club Owners, Club Administrators, and Coaches** use mobile only as Players and use web for authorized management.
- **Platform Administrators** use web for platform oversight and may use mobile as Players.

Club Membership is optional. A Player may belong to no Club, one Club, or multiple Clubs. Club Responsibilities are independent and scoped to each Club.

## Product principles

1. **Player identity is primary.** Club Responsibilities extend a Player; they do not replace the Player identity.
2. **Club boundaries do not trap Players.** Players may form relationships and compete without Club Membership.
3. **Official and social authority remain distinct.** Clubs control Official Tournaments; Players control Challenges and Social Tournaments.
4. **Relationships grant access.** Profile, availability, activity, and competition visibility follow explicit Friendship, Play Group, Club, Tournament, or Guardian relationships.
5. **Youth participation is supervised.** Junior Players use Guardian-controlled access and are not globally discoverable.
6. **Records have explicit authority.** Official Results come from Tournament Organizers; Challenge and Social Tournament results require participant confirmation.
7. **Management stays on web.** Mobile never exposes management mode, even to people with management responsibilities.
8. **Scheduling is informational.** The platform coordinates people and times but does not book courts.
9. **Scope is intentional.** New formats, communication modes, financial workflows, and exceptions require a product decision before implementation.

## Business value

The platform:

- centralizes Club membership and operational responsibility;
- simplifies Player discovery and Club onboarding;
- supports multiple Clubs without mixing Memberships or responsibilities;
- organizes Club activities and Official Tournaments;
- supports trusted Player-organized play and competition;
- preserves coherent competition history and source-specific records.

## Success signals

The product is delivering value when:

- Players can move from Club discovery to an active Membership without offline account administration;
- Club staff can manage members and run an Official Tournament end to end;
- Players can understand who controls an activity or result;
- Junior participation remains Guardian-supervised;
- permissions and history remain correct across multiple Clubs;
- deferred and excluded capabilities do not leak into delivery without an explicit decision.
